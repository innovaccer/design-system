### Reordering List Items

To reorder, pick the item to be reordered via the drag handle placed at the beginning. Items other than the picked item will reorder as soon as the picked item reaches the boundary of the adjacent item.

**Note:** Reordering supports keyboard use as follows:
- Tab and Shift Tab move focus between rows in the list.
- Space picks up the focused row (lift) or drops it at the current position.
- Arrow Down / Arrow Up move the lifted row down or up; when nothing is lifted, focus shifts to the next or previous row. The same applies to **j** and **k**.
- Escape cancels the lift and the row returns to its original position.
- While a row is lifted, Tab and Enter do not move focus out of the list (use Space to drop or Escape to cancel).
