### Maintain Minimum Target Size for Chips

It is recommended to ensure the interactive area of a chip is at least **24 × 24 px**. If the visual chip or its interactive elements are smaller, expand the clickable target area or provide sufficient spacing so the effective target meets the **24 × 24 px minimum target size**, improving accessibility and preventing accidental activation.
