### Maintain Minimum Target Size for Breadcrumb Links

It is recommended to ensure the interactive area of breadcrumb is at least **24 × 24 px**. If the visual link is smaller, provide sufficient spacing or padding so the effective target area meets the **24 × 24 px minimum target size**, improving accessibility and preventing accidental activation.
