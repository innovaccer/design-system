      <td>Focus shifts to the first avatar in the group</td>
    </tr>
    <tr style={{verticalAlign: "top"}}>
      <td>Focus is on a selection avatar</td>
      <td>&#60;Enter&#62; / &#60;Space&#62;</td>
      <td>Selects or deselects the avatar</td>
    </tr>
    <tr style={{verticalAlign: "top"}}>
      <td>Focus is on an avatar or counter</td>
      <td>&#60;Arrow Right&#62; / &#60;Arrow Left&#62; / &#60;Arrow Down&#62; / &#60;Arrow Up&#62;</td>
      <td>Focus shifts to the next or previous avatar or the counter (Note: &#60;Arrow Down&#62; and &#60;Arrow Up&#62; do not move focus if the counter is focused)</td>
    </tr>
    <tr style={{verticalAlign: "top"}}>
      <td></td>
      <td>&#60;Tab&#62; / &#60;Shift Tab&#62;</td>
      <td>Focus shifts away from the avatar group</td>
    </tr>
    <tr style={{verticalAlign: "top"}}>
      <td>Focus is on the counter</td>
      <td>&#60;Enter&#62; / &#60;Space&#62; / &#60;Arrow Down&#62; / &#60;Arrow Right&#62;</td>
      <td>Counter-popover opens and focus shifts to the first focusable item inside the popover</td>
    </tr>
    <tr style={{verticalAlign: "top"}}>
      <td>Focus is inside the counter-popover</td>
      <td>&#60;Enter&#62; / &#60;Space&#62;</td>
      <td>Selects or deselects the focused item (if it is a selection avatar). Popover remains open.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr style={{verticalAlign: "top"}}>
      <td></td>
      <td>&#60;Arrow Down&#62; / &#60;Arrow Right&#62; / &#60;Arrow Up&#62; / &#60;Arrow Left&#62;</td>
      <td>Focus shifts to the next or previous focusable item (including search input), wrapping at the ends</td>
    </tr>
    <tr style={{verticalAlign: "top"}}>
      <td></td>
      <td>&#60;Escape&#62;</td>
      <td>Counter-popover closes and focus shifts to the counter</td>
    </tr>
    <tr style={{verticalAlign: "top"}}>
      <td></td>
      <td>&#60;Tab&#62;</td>
      <td>Focus shifts to the next focusable element. If focus goes out of the popover, popover is closed.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr style={{verticalAlign: "top"}}>
      <td></td>
      <td>&#60;Shift Tab&#62;</td>
      <td>Focus shifts to the previous focusable element. If focus goes out of the popover, popover is closed.</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>
