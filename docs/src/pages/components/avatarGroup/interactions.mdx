      <td>The avatar will be in focus</td>

    <tr style={{verticalAlign: "top"}}>
      <td>Avatar in focus  </td>
      <td>&#60;Tab&#62;</td>
      <td>
        In case of avatar group:
        <ul>
          <li>Shift focus to the next avatar </li>
          <li>In case the focus is on the last avatar, shift focus to next item in Tab sequence on the page. </li>
        </ul>
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr style={{verticalAlign: "top"}}>
      <td></td>
      <td>&#60;Enter&#62;</td>
      <td>
        <ul>
          <li>Select/deselect avatar.</li>
          <li>In case of a numeric avatar, open the popover, and transfer focus from the numeric avatar to either the first focusable item or the search option, depending on the context.</li>
        </ul>
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr style={{verticalAlign: "top"}}>
      <td></td>
      <td>&#60;down arrow&#62;</td>
      <td>
        <ul>
          <li>In case of a numeric avatar, open the popover, and transfer focus from the numeric avatar to either the first focusable item or the search option, depending on the context.</li>
          <li>In case of a default avatar, nothing will happen. </li>
        </ul>
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr style={{verticalAlign: "top"}}>
      <td></td>
      <td>&#60;up arrow&#62;</td>
      <td>
        <ul>
          <li>In case of a numeric avatar, open the popover, and transfer focus from the numeric avatar to the last focusable item.</li>
          <li>In case of a default avatar, nothing will happen.</li>
        </ul>
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr style={{verticalAlign: "top"}}>
      <td></td>
      <td>&#60;Escape&#62;</td>
      <td>Nothing happens.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr style={{verticalAlign: "top"}}>
      <td>Popover is open and an item is in focus<br/>(The trigger will not be in focus at this instance)</td>
      <td>&#60;Tab&#62;</td>
      <td>
        Shift focus to the next item in Tab sequence on the page. <br/>The popover will be excluded from the tab sequence on the page. 
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr style={{verticalAlign: "top"}}>
      <td></td>
      <td>&#60;Enter&#62;</td>
      <td>
        Select the focused item. Popover will remain open. 
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr style={{verticalAlign: "top"}}>
      <td></td>
      <td>&#60;down arrow&#62;</td>
      <td>
        <ul>
          <li>Shift focus to the next focusable item.</li>
          <li>If focus is on the last item, shift focus to the first focusable item.</li>
          <li>
            In case of search: 
            <ul>
              <li class="ml-5">If focus is on the last item, shift focus to search.</li>
              <li class="ml-5">If focus is on search, shift focus to the first focusable item. </li>
            </ul>
          </li>
        </ul>
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr style={{verticalAlign: "top"}}>
      <td></td>
      <td>&#60;up arrow&#62;</td>
      <td>
        <ul>
          <li>Shift focus to the previous focusable item.</li>
          <li>If focus is on the first item, shift focus to the last focusable item.</li>
          <li>
            In case of search: 
            <ul>
              <li class="ml-5">If focus is on the first item, shift focus to search.</li>
              <li class="ml-5">If focus is on search, shift focus to the last focusable item.</li>
            </ul>
          </li>
        </ul>
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr style={{verticalAlign: "top"}}>
      <td></td>
      <td>&#60;Escape&#62;</td>
      <td>Close the popover. Focus is now on the numeric avatar.</td>
    </tr>
