Write a direct subject line to avoid confusion.

<Rules>
  <DOs>
    <InlineMessage>

#### Checklist to remember
<br/>

* Mention what’s attached.
* Always check for spelling, punctuation, and grammar errors before sending it to anyone.
* Write names properly with correct spellings.
* Add hyperlinks in the footer in case your Call To Action is not clickable by the recipient.
