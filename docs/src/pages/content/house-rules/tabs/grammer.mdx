#### Capitalization

Capitalize for right reasons:

<ul>
  <li>
    Capitalize job titles, company or organization name
  </li>
  <li>Proper nouns such as product name and features</li>
  <li>People, country, places</li>
  <li>When you begin a sentence</li>
  <li>Acronyms and initialism</li>
</ul>

Punctuations are saving grace! The language as we know is never ending... So, to put emphasis, on certain words & sentences - stick to the brand house rules. See when, where & how to place them.

#### Tile case

In title case the first letters of the words are capitalized except conjunctions, prepositions and articles. Grammatically speaking the title case has some complex rules when it comes to writing. It generally needs a subjective point of view.

<Rules>
  <DOs>
    <InlineMessage>
      <IconWrapper>To Kill a Mockingbird</IconWrapper>
    </InlineMessage>
    <InlineMessage>
      <IconWrapper>War and Peace</IconWrapper>
    </InlineMessage>
  </DOs>
  <DONTs>
    <InlineMessage>
      <IconWrapper iconType='error'>
        To Kill A Mockingbird
      </IconWrapper>
    </InlineMessage>
    <InlineMessage>
      <IconWrapper iconType='error'>
        War And Peace
      </IconWrapper>
    </InlineMessage>
  </DONTs>
</Rules>
